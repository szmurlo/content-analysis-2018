A thin red rubber band was wrapped around my ankle 3 or 4 times. I pulled it a bit: it sounded like an erhu. I pulled it again; it sounded like strings. I pulled and rubbed it against itself; I got the violins then the whole orchestra. I made a mistake but kept playing through the piece. When I was done, I heard applause.
